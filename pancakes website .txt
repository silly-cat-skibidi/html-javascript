  <h1>Fluffy American Pancakes</h1>

  <img src="https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/silly-cat/images/f/f8/Fatfatmillycat.png/revision/latest?cb=20240612042247" alt="A stack of 5 pancakes smothered in maple syrup" width=30%>
  <br>
<p style="text-align:justify;">This easy <i>American pancake</i> recipe makes light and <q>fluffy</q> pancakes that are <u>great for a weekend brunch</u>. Try adding a handful of fresh blueberries to the batter before cooking.
    <br>
    <br>
    <p><mark>Each serving provides 256 kcal</mark>,7g protein, 35g carbohydrates (of which 9g sugars), <b>9.5g fat (of which 5.5g
    saturates)</b> 1.5 fibre and 1.2g salt. 
  </p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>

 <ul>
 <li>  135g/4 &frac34;oz plain flour </li>
 <li>1 <abbr title="teaspoon">tsp</abbr> baking powder </li>
 <li>&frac12; <abbr title="teaspoon">tsp</abbr> salt </li>
 <li>2 <abbr title="tablespoon">tbsp</abbr> caster sugar </li>
 <li>130ml/4 &frac12;fl oz milk </li>
 <li>1 large egg, lightly beaten</li>
 <li>2 <abbr title="tablespoon">tbsp</abbr> melted butter (allowed to cool slightly) or olive oil, plus extra for cooking </li>
   <br>
   

 </ul>
